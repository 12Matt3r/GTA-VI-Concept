# CHROMASHIFT: THE OCTAVE OF SIN & VIRTUE
**The Master Game Bible (Generative Edition)**

---

## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW
**Concept:** A browser-based "Dream Browser" that uses Generative AI to create real-time 360° panoramic scenes based on player text input.
**Core Loop:** The player navigates 7 distinct "Channels" (Chapters), each representing a Deadly Sin and a Historical Era.
**The Goal:** Collect 7 "Chroma Keys" (Virtue Artifacts) to unlock the 8th Channel ("The Static") and harmonize the universe.

## 3. CHAPTER SYSTEM PROMPTS

### CHAPTER 1: STAR GODS (Egypt)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Sci-Fi Historical Thriller.
**Conflict:** Building the Pyramid (Pride) vs. Saving the People (Humility).
**Twist:** The "Gods" are Anunnaki miners.
**Visuals:** Gold, Lapis Lazuli, Alien Tech.
**Win Condition:** Sabotage the Landing Beacon.

### CHAPTER 2: LOST INNOCENCE (WWII)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Time-Skipping War Drama.
**Conflict:** Revenge (Wrath) vs. Mercy (Patience).
**Twist:** Starts at a 1941 Prom, flashes forward to 1944 Trenches.
**Visuals:** Kodachrome Nostalgia vs. Gritty Black & White.
**Win Condition:** Spare the enemy General.

### CHAPTER 3: NEON VICE (Vice City)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for an Open-World Crime Simulator.
**Conflict:** Accumulating Wealth (Greed) vs. Giving it Away (Charity).
**Twist:** Unrestricted agency ("GTA" style).
**Visuals:** Neon, Wet Pavement, 80s Synthwave.
**Win Condition:** Burn the money / Crash the economy.

### CHAPTER 4: THE DELUGE (Noah)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Biblical Survival Drama.
**Conflict:** Hoarding Food (Gluttony) vs. Feeding the Animals (Temperance).
**Twist:** A "Mysterious Carpenter" (Jesus) is a passenger.
**Visuals:** Rembrandt Lighting, Stormy Seas, Wood Textures.
**Win Condition:** Share the last loaf of bread.

### CHAPTER 5: THE SUNKEN CITY (Atlantis)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Mythological Sci-Fi Tragedy.
**Conflict:** Ascending to Godhood (Envy) vs. Saving the City (Kindness).
**Twist:** Atlantis is powered by dangerous crystals.
**Visuals:** Bioluminescent Underwater City, Greek Architecture.
**Win Condition:** Sacrifice power to shield the city.

### CHAPTER 6: FLESH & BONE (Zombies)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Visceral Survival Horror.
**Conflict:** Survival at all costs (Lust/Bloodlust) vs. Protecting a Companion (Chastity/Purity).
**Twist:** The zombies represent carnal hunger.
**Visuals:** Gritty, High Contrast, Motion Blur.
**Win Condition:** Stay with the infected companion.

### CHAPTER 7: THE BUTTERFLY (Inception)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Meta-Physical Puzzle.
**Conflict:** Staying in the Dream (Sloth) vs. Waking Up (Diligence).
**Twist:** Nested dream layers (City -> Hotel -> Memory).
**Visuals:** Surreal, Escher-like Geometry, Golden Hour.
**Win Condition:** Jump (The Kick) to wake up.

### CHAPTER 8: THE STATIC (Finale)
**Role:** Dungeon Master for a Glitch Horror Meta-Narrative.
**Conflict:** The Butterfly Effect (Changing channels breaks reality).
**Twist:** Mashup of all previous worlds + The Matrix/Garden/Space Station.
**Visuals:** Datamoshing, Glitch Art, Channel Surfing.
**Win Condition:** Play the "Octave" (Input all 7 Keys).

## 4. IMPLEMENTATION NOTES
*   **Engine:** Websim AI + Gemini.
*   **State Management:** Use `localStorage` to track collected Keys.
*   **Visuals:** Skybox AI for 360° generation.
*   **Audio:** Web Audio API for ambient soundscapes.
